Welcome to HabitIt, your personal guide to building life‑changing habits. Below, you’ll find a curated list of habits to boost your health, productivity, and well‑being. Click each one to discover why it matters, how to track it, and what’s at stake if you skip it. Small steps, big wins—start today!

Today’s Focus 🛌 Sleep 7.5–8 h /night, same window, dark‑cold‑quiet room +

Why: Sleep refreshes your mind and body, helping you feel sharp, calm, and ready for the day.

How: Track your sleep with a diary or app. Aim for consistent bedtimes and a cozy, quiet space.

If Not: You might feel worn out, grumpy, or find it hard to focus—like your energy’s running low.

Today’s Focus 🏋️ Lift heavy (compound) + mobility 3×/wk +

Why: Getting stronger keeps your body feeling good and gives you more energy to enjoy life.

How: Keep a simple log of your workouts and try to get a bit stronger each week—small steps add up!

If Not: You might feel stiff, tired, or notice more little aches creeping in.

Today’s Focus 🍎 90 g+ protein, mostly plants & fish; caffeine ≤ 400 mg by 14:00; alcohol < 2 drinks/wk +

Why: Good food keeps you energized, happy, and ready to tackle your day.

How: Check your meals now and then to balance protein and healthy options. Keep caffeine and drinks in check.

If Not: You might feel sluggish, moody, or like your energy’s all over the place.

Today’s Focus 🧠 Deep‑work blocks (2 × 90 min, phone in Faraday pouch) +

Why: Quiet, focused time helps you do your best and feel proud of what you accomplish.

How: Set aside chunks of time for big tasks, no distractions. Track how it goes each week.

If Not: You might feel scattered, busy but not getting much done.

Today’s Focus 🗑️ Digital purge: ruthlessly mute, archive, or delete 90% of inputs +

Why: Clearing digital noise helps you stay calm and keep your mind on what matters.

How: Regularly tidy up your phone or computer—cut out stuff you don’t need.

If Not: You might feel swamped or distracted by too much going on.

Today’s Focus 📓 Weekly 1‑on‑1 with your future self (Roam/Obsidian journal) +

Why: Looking back each week helps you grow and make smarter plans for what’s ahead.

How: Spend 30 minutes writing down what worked, what didn’t, and what’s next.

If Not: You might miss chances to improve or keep running into the same hurdles.

Today’s Focus 🛋️ Therapy or high‑caliber coach, bi‑weekly +

Why: Chatting with someone wise helps you figure things out and feel stronger inside.

How: Book regular talks and be honest about what’s on your mind.

If Not: You might stay stuck on the same worries or feel alone with them.

Today’s Focus 🌄 Novelty adventures 1×/wk (new park, science kit, bike trail) +

Why: New experiences make life fun, spark fresh ideas, and keep you moving.

How: Try something new each week and jot it down. If you’re staying in a lot, plan a little outing.

If Not: Things might feel dull or like you’re in a bit of a slump.

Today’s Focus 🧘 Monthly “monk mode” weekend: off‑grid, hike, or silent retreat +

Why: A quiet break clears your head, lifts your spirits, and brings new thoughts.

How: Plan a calm weekend each month to unplug. Write down any big ideas after.

If Not: You might feel drained or like you’re just going through the motions.

Today’s Focus 💡 Quarterly audit of “S‑curve” skills (e.g., Rust, RL‑HF, multimodal infra) +

Why: Picking up new skills keeps you sharp and opens doors down the road.

How: Every few months, learn something new and try it out—like a small project or note.

If Not: You might feel out of touch or miss out on exciting possibilities.
